Academic Programs

Explore our comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Computer Science and Engineering

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor

B.E. in Computer System Engineering

Duration: 4 years

This undergraduate program provides a strong foundation in computer systems, hardware design, embedded systems, and software development. Students gain practical skills and theoretical knowledge to design, analyze, and innovate in the field of computer engineering.

Admission Requirements
  • Completion of higher secondary education (12th grade or equivalent) with science stream.
  • Minimum aggregate percentage of 60% or equivalent.
  • Entrance examination score (if applicable).

Postgraduate Programs

Master

M.E. in Computer Science and Engineering

Duration: 2 years

This master's program offers advanced study and research opportunities in computer science, software engineering, AI, and related fields. It prepares graduates for leadership roles in academia and industry.

Admission Requirements
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related fields.
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0/4.0 or equivalent.
  • Entrance test score and/or interview (as per university guidelines).
Doctorate

Ph.D. in Computer Science

Duration: 3-5 years

The doctoral program focuses on cutting-edge research in multiple computer science areas. Candidates contribute original knowledge under faculty mentorship and participate in scholarly activities.

Admission Requirements
  • Master's degree in a relevant discipline with a strong academic record.
  • Research proposal and letters of recommendation.
  • Successful interview or entrance evaluation.
