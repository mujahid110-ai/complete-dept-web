Deep Learning Medical Imaging CNN

Hematological Analysis Through Deep Learning

Conducted by Engr. Agha Shiraz A. Khan and Engr. Hudebia Dr. Intesab at the Department of Computer Systems Engineering, QUEST Nawabshah.

Their groundbreaking study, titled "Hematological Analysis Through Deep Learning: An Automated Approach to Peripheral Blood Cells (PBC) Classification", presents a novel automated framework for classifying peripheral blood cells using deep learning techniques such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs).

The research employs transfer learning, data augmentation, and robust dataset engineering to significantly improve diagnostic accuracy and efficiency in hematology, setting new clinical benchmarks for automated analysis.

SARS COVID-19 Detection from X-Rays Using CNNs

A collaborative effort led by Javeriya Bhatti and supervised by ENGR. Agha Shiraz A. Khan demonstrates the use of deep learning in detecting COVID-19 and other pulmonary diseases through X-ray imaging.

Leveraging CNNs on Kaggle and IEEE datasets, the team developed a model that evolved from binary to four-class classification (Covid+, Covid- (Normal), Viral Pneumonia, and Bacterial Pneumonia).
