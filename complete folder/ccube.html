About The Event

The C CUBE 2025 was a two-day technical extravaganza organized by the Computer Systems Engineering Department of Quaid-e-Awam University of Engineering, Science & Technology, Nawabshah. This annual event brought together bright minds to showcase their talents in various technical, creative, and gaming competitions.

Held on April 29-30, 2025, C CUBE provided a platform for students to demonstrate their skills, compete with peers, and celebrate technological innovation. The event featured a diverse range of activities from programming challenges to creative arts and esports tournaments.

Event Highlights:

• 13+ Competitions across technical, creative and gaming categories

• 200+ Participants from various departments and institutions

• Cash Prizes & Certificates for all winners and participants

• Networking Opportunities with peers and faculty
