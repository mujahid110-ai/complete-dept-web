About the Department of Computer Systems Engineering

Department Chair
Prof. Ubaidullah Rajput

Computer Engineering is one of the fastest-growing engineering disciplines, encompassing nearly every aspect of modern life. Being an integral part of our daily lives, computer technology demands highly skilled professionals to keep pace with its rampant growth and innovation.

Keeping in view the significance and impact of this field, the CSE department, as per its mission, strives to develop and maintain a high-quality, comprehensive, rigorous, and accredited teaching program to prepare graduates of competence, conscience, and compassion to excel in the field of computing.

To achieve this goal, we combine the theoretical foundations of computing with practical engineering knowledge vital to industry, providing a broad and integrated curriculum. Equipped with modern state-of-the-art laboratories, trained technical staff, and highly qualified faculty, the Department of Computer Systems Engineering offers the following degree programs:

  • Bachelor of Engineering (Computer Systems Engineering)
  • Master of Engineering (Computer Systems Engineering)
  • Master of Engineering (Computer Communication & Networks)
  • Ph.D. (Computer Systems Engineering)

Our Vision

To produce highly skilled computer engineers who meet the latest trends in computing and contribute effectively to the social, economic, and technological progress of the country.

Our Mission

To provide state-of-the-art education through contemporary techniques in computing disciplines to produce outstanding professionals and better humans who can contribute effectively to national progress and advance computing knowledge worldwide.
