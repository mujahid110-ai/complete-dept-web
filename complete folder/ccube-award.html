C Cube Team Wins National Award

We are proud to announce that our C Cube team won first prize in the National Level Programming Contest hosted by ABC University. Competing against top institutions, our students demonstrated exceptional skills in coding, design, and problem-solving.

The winning project focused on building a smart attendance system using facial recognition and was praised for its practicality and innovation.

This achievement reflects the dedication of our students and the quality of mentorship provided by our faculty.
