Inauguration of AI & ML Lab

Our department proudly inaugurated a state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Lab aimed at advancing research and innovation in emerging technologies.

The lab is equipped with high-performance computing systems, GPUs, and modern tools for data science, deep learning, and robotics.

This initiative empowers students and researchers to work on real-world projects and stay ahead in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
