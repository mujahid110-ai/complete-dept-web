Outcome-Based Education (OBE)

A modern educational approach focusing on measuring student performance outcomes

Our OB-based degree program in Computer Systems Engineering is designed to ensure that graduates achieve specific, measurable outcomes. This approach focuses on what students can actually do after they complete their education, rather than just what they were taught.

Assessment Process

Direct Assessment

Evaluation through exams, quizzes, projects, and presentations that directly measure student achievement of CLOs.

Indirect Assessment

Surveys, interviews, and alumni feedback that provide additional insights into program effectiveness.

Continuous Improvement

Regular review of assessment data to identify areas for improvement and update curriculum accordingly.
